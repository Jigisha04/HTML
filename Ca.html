Featured Recipe: Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1-3/4 cups flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1-1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1-1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Cooking Time:

35 minutes

Detailed Recipe Information: Chocolate Cake

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, stir together the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla, and beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in the boiling water (batter will be thin).
  5. Pour the batter into prepared pans.
  6. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  7. Cool in pans for 10 minutes, then remove to wire racks to cool completely.

Additional Cooking Tips & Resources

  • Tip: Use room temperature ingredients for a smoother batter.
